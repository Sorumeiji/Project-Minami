My Process

With consistancy and detail, each commission is met with a plan to ensure the work brings satsifaction to meet standards.

Communication

Communication about the commission during and after the proces is the most imporant step to take. It ensures the commission to meet the needs of the client.

Initial Sketch

After reviewing the commission, and gathering references, and research. A non-colored rough sketch is done. To capture preliminary ideas.

Line Art + Color

When the sketch is approved, the line art is done to define the shape,figure, emotions and other elements of the art. Then the coloring is done after.

Polish + Delivery

The polish is completed after the line art, coloring and detail are added. With approval the commission is deliver to the client.
